# Compliance Documents — Entity Pack

This folder contains the per-entity compliance documents required by the
MLRO for the April 2026 reporting cycle and the firm's ongoing AML/CFT/CPF
programme.

Six entities are covered. Each entity has its own copy of every document.
Do not treat one entity's file as a template for another. The facts,
risk profile, and operational scope differ.

## Entities

1. Fine Gold LLC (`fine-gold-llc`)
2. Fine Gold Branch (`fine-gold-branch`)
3. Gramaltin AS (`gramaltin-as`)
4. Naples LLC (`naples-llc`)

## MLRO

Primary MLRO: Luisa Fernanda. All documents are issued under her authority
and signed by her in the first instance, with countersignature by the
Compliance Officer and, where required, the Board.

## Placeholders

Any text in square brackets (for example `[Trade Licence No.]`,
`[Registered Address]`, `[Backup MLRO Full Legal Name]`) must be filled in
by the MLRO before the document is presented for signature. These are not
oversights; they are fields that can only be answered from the firm's
internal records.

## Regulatory basis

All documents cite the UAE AML/CFT/CPF framework in force on the date of
issue: Federal Decree-Law No. 10 of 2025 (FDL 10/2025), Cabinet Resolution
134/2025, Cabinet Resolution 74/2020, Cabinet Resolution 156/2025, Cabinet
Decision 109/2023, Cabinet Resolution 71/2024, and MoE Circular 08/AML/2021.
Sector guidance from FATF Recommendations 22 and 23, LBMA Responsible Gold
Guidance v9, and the UAE MoE Responsible Sourcing of Gold Framework is
applied where relevant to the precious-metal trading entities.

## Review cycle

Every document in this pack is reviewed at least once a year and within
30 days of any new MoE circular or federal amendment, whichever comes
first (Cabinet Resolution 134/2025 Art.19). The review date is recorded
in the document footer.
