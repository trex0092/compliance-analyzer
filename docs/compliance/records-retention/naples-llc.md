# Records Retention Schedule — Naples LLC

Issuing officer: Luisa Fernanda, MLRO
Document date: 16/04/2026
Review date: 16/04/2027 or within 30 days of any new MoE circular
Version: 1.0

## 1. Scope

Naples LLC is the firm's trading entity with a concentration in
wholesale metal purchases from walk-in and established suppliers. It
sees a higher proportion of cash and physical-delivery transactions
than the group's cross-border entities, and therefore attracts the
MoE Circular 08/AML/2021 thresholds most frequently. This schedule
gives extra weight to cash reporting evidence, supplier onboarding
files, and the contemporaneous notes that support cash-threshold
decisions.

The schedule covers every record generated by Naples LLC in connection
with suppliers, customers, cash and non-cash transactions, sanctions
screening, filings, training, and internal controls. It applies across
the firm's digital estate (HAWKEYE STERLING, ERP, Asana, GitHub,
Netlify Blobs, Anthropic) and any physical records kept on the
premises.

## 2. Data categories

1. Supplier and customer KYC including provenance evidence for bought
   metal.
2. Transactions, split between cash, card, bank transfer, and
   bullion-for-bullion exchanges.
3. Cash thresholds — AED 55,000 CTR trigger tracking for DPMS, supplier
   aggregation notes, attempted-split detection records.
4. Sanctions and PEP screenings for both customers and suppliers.
5. STR, CTR, DPMSR, and any other goAML filings and their supporting
   evidence.
6. Audit logs, four-eyes approvals, policy versions, training records,
   brain telemetry.

## 3. Storage location by category

| Category | Primary | Secondary / mirror |
|---|---|---|
| Supplier / customer KYC | Internal file server | HAWKEYE Netlify Blobs |
| Transactions | ERP with cash-flag index | HAWKEYE case file |
| Cash threshold tracking | HAWKEYE CTR module | CTR submission archive |
| Screenings | HAWKEYE STERLING database | Netlify Blobs snapshot |
| Filings | goAML archive | HAWKEYE case file, Asana task |
| Audit logs | HAWKEYE append-only log | Weekly export to Netlify Blobs |
| Four-eyes approvals | HAWKEYE signature store | Audit-log cross-reference |
| Brain telemetry | HAWKEYE run database | Anthropic tenant logs (30 days) |

Paper records (till slips, counter notes, delivery notes) are scanned
and attached to the ERP transaction on the day they are produced. The
paper copy is then retained in a dated box file at the Naples LLC
office and destroyed only once the scan has been validated and the
ten-year period has expired.

## 4. Retention period

Ten years minimum, per Article 24 of FDL No. 10 of 2025. Because
Naples LLC generates the largest share of the firm's cash transactions,
the entity maintains a dedicated index of AED 55,000 threshold events
that spans the full ten-year window so that MoE inspection teams can
audit the series in one place.

## 5. Legal basis

Article 24 of FDL No. 10 of 2025 is the floor. MoE Circular 08/AML/2021
applies directly because the entity is a DPMS and handles cash. The
lawful basis for processing personal data is UAE PDPL Article 7
(compliance with a legal obligation). Cabinet Resolution 134/2025
Article 19 drives the internal review cycle.

## 6. Deletion authority

No record is deleted before the ten-year floor. Deletion requires
written proposal from the MLRO, countersignature from the Compliance
Officer, and a note from the Head of Operations confirming no open
supplier dispute or inventory reconciliation touches the record.

## 7. Disposal procedure

Digital records: cryptographic key erasure followed by a hard delete
and a certified audit entry. Paper records: cross-cut shredding with
a two-officer witness and a destruction certificate; the same box
file is destroyed as a whole unit so no partial destruction occurs.

## 8. Legal hold

A hold is triggered by any active investigation, MoE inspection
notice, STR, or freeze under Cabinet Resolution 74/2020. The hold is
applied both to the HAWKEYE record and to the associated paper box
file, and is recorded in the audit log. Staff are reminded in writing
that a hold is in place.

## 9. Cash-specific notes

For every cash transaction at or above AED 55,000 (either single or
aggregated over a 30-day window for one customer), the firm retains,
inside the ten-year window: the contemporaneous cash-handling note,
the DPMSR submission receipt from goAML, the MLRO review note, and
the four-eyes approval record if one was required. Attempted-split
events where a customer appears to be breaking a transaction below the
threshold are documented separately with the MLRO's written assessment
and retained for the same ten-year period whether or not a filing
resulted.
