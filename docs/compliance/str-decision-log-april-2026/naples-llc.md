## 1. Purpose of this log

This log records the MLRO's consideration of any matter originating
at Naples LLC that could have given rise to a STR or SAR during
April 2026. Because Naples LLC is the group's cash-intensive DPMS
entity, the log also confirms that structuring red flags, attempted-
split events, and cash-threshold decisions were reviewed during the
period.

## 2. Regulatory basis

Federal Decree-Law No. 10 of 2025, Articles 15 and 16 (consideration
of suspicion), 26 and 27 (filing), 29 (tipping off), 24 (retention).
MoE Circular 08/AML/2021 (DPMS cash transaction reporting threshold
AED 55,000). Cabinet Resolution 134/2025 Article 19 (annual internal
review).

## 3. Entries for the reporting period — STR consideration

No STR or SAR filings were made in respect of Naples LLC during
April 2026. No matter was escalated to the MLRO that in her
judgement met the threshold for STR filing. Structuring red flags
were reviewed weekly against the aggregation report and no
confirmed case was identified during the period.

## 4. Nil declaration

"No suspicious activity was identified or considered by the MLRO in
respect of Naples LLC during the month of April 2026 that met the
threshold for a STR or SAR filing. This nil declaration confirms the
STR reporting obligation under Articles 15 and 16 of UAE Federal
Decree-Law No. 10 of 2025 was actively reviewed and discharged."

## 5. Controls exercised during the reporting period

a) Two-officer cash-count rule applied to every cash transaction.
b) AED 55,000 DPMS threshold enforcement at every till via HAWKEYE
STERLING.
c) Weekly aggregation review for 30-day customer cash totals
approaching the threshold.
d) Structuring and attempted-split monitoring against the HAWKEYE
red-flag rules.
e) Full-list sanctions screening on every named counterparty and
every supplier.
f) RF-R16 old-gold intake controls on every walk-in seller.

## 6. Related DPMSR activity for the period

DPMSR filings and cash-threshold events, whether or not they led to
a STR, are recorded in the entity's goAML Filing Reconciliation for
April 2026 (separate document in this pack). This STR decision log
does not duplicate those entries; it only records STR consideration.

## 7. Retention

Retained for ten years under FDL Article 24, as a Naples LLC record
per the entity's retention schedule.

## 8. Tipping-off reminder (FDL Article 29)

The tipping-off prohibition applies. Counter staff were briefed in
the April training refresher that any AML-related enquiry from a
customer or third party is routed to the MLRO without substantive
response.
