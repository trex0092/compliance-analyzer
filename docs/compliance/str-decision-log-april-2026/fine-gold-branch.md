## 1. Purpose of this log

This log records the MLRO's consideration of any matter originating
at Fine Gold Branch that could have given rise to an STR, SAR, or
related filing during the reporting period. The Branch does not
file in its own name; a matter that crossed the filing threshold
would be filed at parent level (Fine Gold LLC) with the Branch
involvement recorded in the filing narrative. This log exists so the
Branch's own consideration of suspicion is visible to inspectors
without having to reconstruct it from the parent's records.

## 2. Regulatory basis

Federal Decree-Law No. 10 of 2025, Articles 15 and 16 (consideration
of suspicion), 26 and 27 (filing), 29 (tipping off), and 24 (ten-year
retention). Cabinet Resolution 134/2025 Article 19 (annual internal
review of the adequacy of the firm's controls, including this log).

## 3. Entries for the reporting period

No entries. No matter originating at the Branch was escalated to the

full reporting period.
b) Sanctions screening against UN / OFAC / EU / UK / UAE / EOCN lists
on all Branch counterparties on onboarding and at review cadence.
c) PEP and adverse-media screen at Branch level.
d) Branch-to-MLRO escalation channel staffed and tested.

applicable.
