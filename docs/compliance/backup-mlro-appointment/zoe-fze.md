## 2. Reason for appointment

Zoe FZE operates inside a UAE free zone. Its AML/CFT/CPF duties
include both the federal framework and any free-zone authority
requirements. Stock movements through the free-zone perimeter cannot
pause for an MLRO to become reachable; the Backup exists so that a
screening clearance or a freeze decision can be made without delay.

Legal basis: FDL No. 10 of 2025 Article 20; Cabinet Resolution
134/2025 Articles 18 and 19; the free-zone authority's own MLRO
requirements where applicable.

## 4. Scope of authority during activation

When active, the Backup MLRO has authority to:

a) Receive suspicion reports and decide on filing.
b) File STR, SAR, CTR, CNMR and any free-zone authority
notifications.
c) Execute a freeze within 24 clock hours on a confirmed sanctions
match under Cabinet Resolution 74/2020.
d) Notify the Executive Office for AML/CFT and, in parallel, the
free-zone authority where required.
e) Approve or decline EDD onboarding.
f) Halt a stock movement at the free-zone gate pending screening
clearance or a risk review.
g) Direct the Licensing Officer on a free-zone authority response.

The appointee does not have authority to amend policy or to change
the risk appetite.

## 5. Activation triggers

As for Fine Gold LLC, with an added specific trigger: if the free-
zone authority raises an urgent question on a specific stock movement
and the primary MLRO is not reachable within one business hour, the
Backup steps in to respond.

## 6. Deactivation

Automatic on resolution. Handover note covers every filing, every
stock-movement decision at the gate, and every free-zone authority
communication.

## 7. Reporting and handover

Retained for ten years under FDL Art.24. The handover note also
covers any interaction with the free-zone authority during the
active period so that a consistent record can be produced on request.

## 8. Regulatory notification

Cabinet Resolution 134/2025 Article 18 notification to the MoE
within 30 days of Board signature. The free-zone authority is
notified in parallel where its procedures require.
