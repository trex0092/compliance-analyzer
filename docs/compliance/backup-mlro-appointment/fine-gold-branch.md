## 2. Reason for appointment

Fine Gold Branch does not hold separate legal personality and
therefore does not appoint its own MLRO. The firm-wide MLRO and her
Backup cover Branch activity. This instrument formalises that
coverage so Branch staff know whom to escalate to and so the parent
Board's appointment decision is documented at Branch level for
inspection purposes.

The legal basis for the appointment is FDL No. 10 of 2025 Article 20
(requirement for an MLRO to be available at all times) and Cabinet
Resolution 134/2025 Article 18 (notification of changes in MLRO
arrangements). Cabinet Resolution 134/2025 Article 19 drives the
annual review of this instrument.

## 4. Scope of authority during activation

When this appointment is active, the Backup MLRO acts with the same
authority as the primary MLRO in respect of Branch activity. In
particular:

a) Receives suspicion reports from Branch staff and decides on
filing.
b) Files through goAML under the parent's registration, indicating
in the filing narrative that the matter originates at Branch level.
c) Executes a freeze within 24 clock hours on a confirmed sanctions
match under Cabinet Resolution 74/2020.
d) Notifies the Executive Office for AML/CFT where required.
e) Halts any Branch transaction pending review.

The appointee does not hold authority to change either the parent or
the Branch policy, to alter the firm's risk appetite, or to file in
the Branch's own name (the Branch does not file in its own name).

## 5. Activation triggers

Identical to the parent's instrument. Activation is shared between
the two documents: one event activates the Backup across both parent
and Branch scope simultaneously.

## 6. Deactivation

Identical to the parent's instrument. Automatic on trigger
resolution, with a written handover note covering both parent and
Branch activity during the period of absence.

## 7. Reporting and handover

A single handover note covers both parent and Branch activity. The
Branch CO co-signs the Branch section of the note so that Branch
matters are specifically acknowledged. Retained for ten years under
FDL Art.24.

## 8. Regulatory notification

Cabinet Resolution 134/2025 Article 18 notification is filed by the
Compliance Officer at parent level and references both parent and
Branch scope. The receipt is retained at parent level and a copy is
held at Branch level.
