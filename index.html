🎯 NORAD War Room — real-time compliance command centre
FDL Art.20-21 situational awareness · Cabinet Res 134/2025 Art.19 continuous monitoring · EOCN Inspection Manual §9
What is the War Room?
The NORAD War Room is the live operational view of the entire compliance estate. It opens a server-sent-events (SSE) stream from /api/warroom/stream and pushes real-time situational awareness snapshots to the dashboard without manual refresh. It is built for the MLRO + CO + ops officer on shift — so they can react to incidents in seconds, not after an end-of-day report.
What you'll see when you click ▶ Start Live
⚡ Live Threat Feed
Sanctions hits, structuring patterns, EOCN watch-list pings, and STR triggers as they happen — colour-coded by severity (CRITICAL / HIGH / MEDIUM).
🛰 SLA Countdown
Live countdowns for every regulatory deadline currently running: 24h asset freeze, 5-day CNMR, 10-day STR, 15-day UBO re-verify.
🧠 Brain Telemetry
Hot subsystem stack — which of the 100+ brain subsystems are firing right now (sanctions screen, ESG scorer, anomaly explainer, AI governance audit, etc.).
📨 Asana Bridge
Mirrors the auto-created Asana tasks (STR draft, CDD review, sanctions response) so you can see them flow without leaving the SPA.
🔒 Four-Eyes Queue
Approval requests waiting on a second approver — pending count, oldest item age, breaches of the 24h SLA.
🌍 Geo Heatmap
High-risk jurisdictions touched by today's transactions, weighted by FATF list status and Wolfsberg country score.
Why it exists
Compliance evidence has historically been retrospective — written up after the fact, in a Word document, days after the trigger event. EOCN, MoE and LBMA inspectors increasingly expect live evidence of continuous monitoring per Cabinet Res 134/2025 Art.19. The War Room is that live evidence: press 🔊 Brief at any time and the voice assistant will read the current state aloud, which is what an inspector sees during a walkthrough.

Click ▶ Start Live to open the SSE stream and begin rendering real-time war-room snapshots.
