## 1. Purpose of this Report

This report is submitted by the Money Laundering Reporting Officer (the "MLRO") to senior management and to the Board of the above-named entity in discharge of the duties set out under Federal Decree-Law No. 10 of 2025 on the Criminalisation of Money Laundering, the Financing of Terrorism and the Financing of Illegal Organisations (the "AML/CFT/CPF Law"), Cabinet Resolution No. 134 of 2025 (the "Implementing Regulations"), Cabinet Resolution No. 74 of 2020 concerning Targeted Financial Sanctions, Cabinet Resolution No. 156 of 2025 on Proliferation Financing and Dual-Use Controls, Cabinet Decision No. 109 of 2023 on the Beneficial Ownership Register, and the Ministry of Economy Circular No. 08/AML of 2021 addressed to Dealers in Precious Metals and Stones.

It is intended to provide a complete, factual and proportionate account of the entity's financial crime exposure during the reporting period, the control activities performed by the compliance function, the open risks that remain under management, and the MLRO's professional judgement on the overall adequacy of the entity's anti-financial-crime framework. The report is to be read alongside the underlying working papers, the goAML submissions log, the screening register and the customer due diligence files, all of which are retained for not less than ten years in accordance with Article 24 of the AML/CFT/CPF Law.
