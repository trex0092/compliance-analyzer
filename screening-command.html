Sanctions-list coverage

Parallel screen against six lists: UN Consolidated (1267/1988/1989/2253), OFAC SDN, OFAC Consolidated (non-SDN), EU CFSP, UK OFSI (HMT), and UAE EOCN. UAE EOCN and UN are mandatory and cannot be deselected.

Matching engine

Five-algorithm ensemble — Jaro-Winkler, Levenshtein, Soundex, Double Metaphone, token-set — catching phonetic variants, transliterations, word-order permutations, and single-character edits. Deterministic only; no LLM in the match path.

Risk scoring

Every hit feeds an explainable Bayesian risk score with an open-source adverse-media sweep (PEP, litigation, fraud, terrorism-financing signals) on the same pass.

Automation

Confirmed or potential matches auto-enrol into the daily watchlist (06:00 / 14:00 UTC cron). A tagged Asana task lands in the SCREENINGS project, assigned to the on-duty MLRO.

MLRO attestation

MLRO closes every event with a disposition (negative / false positive / partial / confirmed), a named reviewer, and a ≥ 20-character rationale — creating an append-only audit record for MoE, LBMA, and internal audit.

Mandatory UAE Lists

Cabinet Decision No.(74)/2020. These two lists are legally mandatory for all UAE reporting entities (DPMS, DNFBP, FI). Failure to screen against either list constitutes a regulatory offence under FDL No.10/2025 Art.35 and is subject to administrative penalty under Cabinet Res 71/2024 (AED 10K–100M range).

Enhanced Controls

EOCN Guidance. For non-UAE unilateral / multilateral lists, consult your supervisory authority for appropriate course of action. Opting out is permitted; every opt-out is logged with the screening event (FDL Art.24 — 10-year retention).

Risk Categories

Content categories screened against every selected list + adverse-media corpus. Default: all ON. Turning a category OFF is logged on the screening event (FDL Art.24 — 10-year retention).

Screen a Subject

Runs multi-modal fuzzy matching (Jaro-Winkler + Levenshtein + Soundex + Double Metaphone + token-set) against every selected list, searches adverse media, and computes an explainable risk score. Regulatory basis: FDL Art.12-14, 20-21, 24, 26-27, 29; Cabinet Res 134/2025 Art.7-10, 14, 19; Cabinet Res 74/2020 Art.4-7.

Every screened subject is automatically enrolled in daily monitoring (06:00 / 14:00 UTC) and in immediate sanctions / adverse-media / PEP / UBO alerts. No opt-out (FDL Art.20-21, Cabinet Res 134/2025 Art.19).

Four-eyes approval required

Partial and confirmed matches cannot be saved by a single reviewer. A second, independent approver must attest to the outcome before the record is written to the audit log (FDL No.10/2025 Art.20-21; Cabinet Res 134/2025 Art.19 — internal review). The first reviewer and the second approver must be different people.

Critical alerts auto-create an Asana task. FDL Art.15-16, 26-27; Cabinet Res 134/2025 Art.16, 19.
